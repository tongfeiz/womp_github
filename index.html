FAQ

  • Do I need a new mattress?
    Nope. Womp works with the one you already have. Just strap it on and sleep like usual. We’ll handle the rest.
  • How strong are the vibrations?
    Our vibrating motors are roughly 10x stronger than the ones in your phone. They’re built to move you, not buzz politely.
  • What happens if I ignore it or don't get up?
    You’re welcome to try. Womp starts calm, but escalates until you’re up and moving. For the truly stubborn, we have a powerful speaker as backup.
  • Won’t this just make me more tired?
    The opposite. womp eliminates snooze spirals and get you up exactly when you need. Down the road, it’ll even learn your sleep patterns to help you wake up smarter. The less you need us, the better we’ve done our job.
